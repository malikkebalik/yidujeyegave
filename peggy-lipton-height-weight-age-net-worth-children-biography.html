    Peggy Lipton was an American actor, model, singer, musician, and stage artist who is known for her roles like playing Julie Barnes in the ABC crime drama series The Mod Squad, Hadley Larson in the CBS drama series Angel Falls, and Norma Jennings in the ABC mystery drama series Twin Peaks. She was also cast in many other shows such as Rules of Engagement, Cuts, Bewitched, The Alfred Hitchcock Hour, The Virginian, There’s… Johnny!, The Invaders, The Hitchhiker, House of Lies, Angie Tribeca, and Psych. Moreover, her film credits include War Party, Purple People Eater, The Intern, Fatal Charm, True Identity, Twin Peaks: Fire Walk with Me, When in Rome, Jackpot, Skipped Parts, and A Dog’s Purpose.
