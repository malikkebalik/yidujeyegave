    Jermell Charlo Professional Achievements

    After a decade of relentless effort, Jermell Charlo clinched his first title, the WBC light middleweight title, on May 21, 2016, in a match against John Jackson. He defended this title successfully in three consecutive matches before losing to Tony Harrison on December 22, 2018.

He also defended the WBC light middleweight title and acquired the WBA (Super), IBF, and vacant The Ring light middleweight titles in a spectacular match against Jeison Rosario on September 26, 2020.
