    While this is true for almost all famous children, it is especially true for Helene Boshoven Samuel (aka Leni). She is supermodel Heidi Klum’s eldest child from her brief union with Italian business tycoon Flavio Briatore.

    Her mother Heidi gave birth to her without the presence of her biological father after her parents divorced before she was born.

    On May 4, 2004, Helene was born in New York City. When her daughter Leni was born, her mother was with her then-boyfriend, the renowned British artist Henry Olusegun Adeola Samuel, also known as Seal. But when Helene’s kid was born, her biological father wasn’t present.

    After starting dating in March 2003, Klum and her ex-boyfriend Flavio called it quits shortly after the model revealed her first pregnancy in December of that same year. Briatore claimed that after their breakup, their distance from one another “was unbridgeable” because Heidi lived in Los Angeles and he in London.

    The supermodel Klum, who was born in Germany, said her agent tells People that the baby weighed seven pounds. 5 ounces. “Mother and daughter are feeling great,” the insider continued. Additionally, a source stated that Briatore and Heidi got along well and that “they will be raising the child together as friends.”

    Indeed, Helene is adopted by Seal, the second husband of her mother. The couple’s romantic relationship started when Klum split up with her ex-boyfriend Flavio.

    Before getting engaged on a glacier in Whistler, British Columbia in December 2004, the couple dated for several months. Speaking of weddings, Heidi and Seal spoke their vows on May 10, 2005, so that day didn’t take too long. The couple planned their Mexican beach wedding.

    Heidi had three children with Seal. In September 2005, Henry Günther Ademola Dashtu Samuel, a son, became their first child. November 2006 saw the birth of their second son, Johan Riley Fyodor Taiwo Samuel.

    In addition, in October 2009, the couple welcomed Lou Sulola Samuel, a daughter, as their third child. On November 21st of the same year, Heidi’s first daughter Helene was also lawfully adopted by the British artist

    Speaking with Italy’s Il Corriere Della Sera about his daughter’s adoption, her biological father Flavio Briatore stated,

    “A child needs to grow up in a family, so even though Leni is my biological daughter, the three of us happily agreed that it made more sense if Seal adopted her.”

    In the same interview, he discussed his relationships with Seal and Heidi as well.

    “Seal, Heidi, and I have developed a wonderful bond.”

    The businessman went on to say,

    It’s difficult to miss a baby you never get to see. However, I am aware that Leni is not a mistreated child.

    Speaking more about his connection with his daughter Lene, 70-year-old Flavio said that although they spoke on the phone for two hours every day, “it wasn’t enough.” She had to remain with her mother.

    What’s the True Name of Her?

    On the internet, there is a lot of misunderstanding over her true name. According to several accounts, Heidi’s daughter was born with the last name Helen Klum, but according to other accounts, when Seal formally adopted her in 2009, her last name changed to Samuel.

    In an interview with Vogue in December 2020, model Heidi, however, ended any rumors about her daughter’s true identity. She revealed that Leni Olumi Klum, not Helene Boshoven Samuel, is her name.

    In April 2012, Klum filed for divorce from Seal, and in October 2015, their divorce was finalized. However, Leni and her adoptive father continue to have a strong relationship even after their split.

    Heidi herself has said, rather sympathetically, that Seal, not Flavio, is her father.

    After years apart, Helene finally met her biological father.

    Despite the fact that Briatore had little influence in Leni’s life, the father-daughter connection has made headlines in the past, but that all changed in July 2018 when the two met at the Cala di Volpe hotel in Porto Cervo. They also gave each other embraces, according to TMZ.

    The newspaper was also informed by a source that Klum relocated there with her parents and her three other children from her previous marriage to Seal. Flavio, her former beau, joined them all and they had dinner together.

    Her Ascenting Career in Modeling

    Yes, the sixteen-year-old daughter is doing as her mother does. Modeling was always Leni’s destiny. At the age of twelve, she was given the chance to be on the cover of a clothing and accessory business named Brandy Melville. The model had to decline the offer even though it was one of her favorite brands since her mother wouldn’t let her.

    The first modeling job Helene Boshoven Samuel had with her mother Heidi.

    After her first picture session for the January edition of Vogue Germany in December 2020, Leni made her debut in the modeling industry. The image of Helene and her mother Heidi was taken by photographer Chris Colls for Vogue Germany. Leni further made an appearance at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in January 2021.

    Helene is interested in dancing in addition to modeling. Regarding her oldest child’s interest in the glam world, her mother spoke with Hello! Magazine in 2018.

    She’s itching to dance now. She is free to do as she pleases. I’m not planting any ideas about right and wrong in my children’s minds. I want them to think up ideas on their own. They’re not exactly theater goers.

    How Was Her Love Life? Is There A Boyfriend For Helene?

    The model hasn’t disclosed anything about her romantic life to the public, although one Vogue story said that Helene desired a matching tattoo of her partner in her lips. Her mother allegedly forbade her daughter from doing that, though.

    Hopefully, the couple in hiding will soon declare their romance public.

    Has The Adjacent Friendship Tattoo With Her Brother Bill And Her Stepfather Tom Kaulitz

    Leni has a close relationship with her stepfather, musician Tom Kaulitz of Tokio Hotel. Additionally, she has the same tattoo on the inside of her fingers as her stepfather Tom and his brother Bill had. Three little dots are shown on their friendship tattoo.

    Her Wealth

    Leni is living the life of luxury as the daughter of a supermodel. However, it is still unknown how much money the young girl is worth. Her mother Heidi, on the other hand, is worth $160 million.
