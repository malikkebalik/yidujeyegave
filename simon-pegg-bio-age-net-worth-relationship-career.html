    Where was Simon Pegg born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

    Simon Pegg was born in Brockworth, Gloucestershire, on February 14, 1970. John Beckingham, a musician and businessman, and Gillian Rosemary reared him in Brockworth, Gloucestershire.

    He had to change schools, and he completed his coursework at Brockworth Comprehensive Secondary School and The King’s School in Gloucester.
