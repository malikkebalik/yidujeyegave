    The Little Mermaid sister names and pictures are Perla, Indira, Karina, Caspia, Mala and Tamika. All of Ariel sisters are from diverse backgrounds.

    The fans of the movie were happy with the changes in the name of Ariel's sisters. They found it a more interesting as the new names are easy-to-remember.

    Similarly, as the release date is approaching near, fans cannot wait to see the new as well as a revised character on the big screen.

    Ariel sisters names live-action are Perla, Indira, Karina, Caspia, Mala, and Tamika.

    1. Perla

    Perla is a tall fair looking mermaid with braided hair and red and whitetails. She is referred to as the princess of Atlantica.

    The Mermaid character Perla is based on Andrina from the original The Little Mermaid Film.

    Perla is the sixth daughter of King Triton, She is the older sister to Ariel and the youngest of four sisters.

    2. Indira

    Indira is a long-haired dark colored mermaid with a yellow mermaid tail. She is also one of Ariel's Mermaid-sisters.

    The Character Indira is based on Alana from the original Animated Mermaid movie.

    Indira is the second born of the family and is the younger sister to Mala and older to Caspia, Tamika, Karina, Perla, and Ariel.

    3. Karina

    Karina is a beautiful Mermaid with a blue tail with white ponytail hair.

    The Mermaid Karina is based on Arista from the original The Little Mermaid Film.

    Karina is the fifth-born daughter of King Triton and the older sister to Perla and Ariel.

    4. Caspia

    Caspia is a beautiful pink hair Mermaid with a dark blue and orange tail.

    Caspia is based on the character Adella from the original Mermaid movie.

    Caspia is the third Mermaid daughter of the King of Atlantica. She is the youngest sister of Mala and Indira.

    5. Mala

    Mala is an Asian-inspired beautiful mermaid with long hair and a pink mermaid tail.

    Mala is the new version of Aquata from the original old mermaid movie. She is the fourth-born daughter of King Triton.

    Mala is the firstborn of King Triton and Queen Athena.

    6. Tamika

    Tamika is a beautiful dark-skinned Mermaid with afro-style hair and a mixed-colored mermaid tail.

    She is the middle daughter of King Triton and the older sister to Karina, Perla, and Ariel.

    She is based on Attina from the original Little Mermaid movie.
