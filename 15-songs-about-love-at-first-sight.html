    15 Songs About Love At First Sight

    Love is a beautiful feeling and when one is all caught up in such feeling, everything seems blissful. It is even more interesting and lovely when the person you love, loves you back in the same way and with equal energy.

    13 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Love is a beautiful feeling and when one is all caught up in such feeling, everything seems blissful. It is even more interesting and lovely when the person you love, loves you back in the same way and with equal energy.

    Love songs are musical stories people in love or lovers tell to try to explain what they feel and how they feel it. The messages could be direct, to the person’s lover or it could be for public consumption.

    Love songs have been around since time immemorial. It gets redefined as humans and their feelings evolve, however, it is a universal language.

    In this article today, we will take a look at some love at first sight songs. Not just any love songs, those ones that depict what you may have felt when you saw someone for the first time and you ‘zing’ with the person.

    We will share 15 of those beautiful love at first sight songs that try to explain how you must have felt at the instant.

    Table of Contents

    Here Are 15 Songs About Falling In Love At First Sight

    1. James Arthur – “Say You Won’t Let Go”

    This song by James Arthur is about his encounter with a lady he met and instantly realized was his better half. They both met and sparked things off, dancing and drinking happily together, the first time they met.

    Lyrics

    I knew I loved you then, but you’d never know

    ‘Cause I played it cool

    When I was scared of letting go

    I knew I needed you, but I never showed

    But I wanna stay with you

    Until we’re grey and old

    Just say you won’t let go, just say you won’t let go

    2. Savage Garden – “ I knew I Loved You’

    This is a song by the Australian group comprising of two guys who go by the name ‘Savage Garden’.

    This song about love at first sight tells the story of a guy who found a lover and best friend in the same lady whom he saw for the very first time.

    Lyrics

    I knew I loved you before I met you

    I think I dreamed you into life

    I knew I loved you before I met you

    I have been waiting all my life

    I am complete now that I found you

    3. Carly Rae Jepsen – “Call Me Maybe”

    This song is the tale of a girl who falls deeply in love with a boy whom she met for the first time. The song tells about how she wants to get closer to him as she has fallen in love with him and would want to build a relationship.

    Lyrics

    Hey I just met you and this is crazy

    But here’s my number, so call me maybe

    It’s hard to look right at you, baby

    But here’s my number, so call me maybe

    4. Kylie Minogue – “Love At First Sight”

    The story is about how meeting the love of your life can turn a bad day to good. With just one look, it was love at first sight and the blue day became bright and beautiful.

    Lyrics

    Thought that I was going crazy

    Just having one of those days yeah

    Didn’t know what to do

    Then there was you

    What man wouldn’t want a stunning and elegant lady dancing with him? To crown it up, the lady is wearing red; the color of blood which is pumped by the heart itself.
