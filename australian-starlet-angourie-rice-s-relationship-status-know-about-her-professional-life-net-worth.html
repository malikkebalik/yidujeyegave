    The Australian actress portrayed the role of a supporting character as Elizabeth "Betty" Brant in the popular film. In today's article, let's discuss Angourie Rice's early professional and personal life in detail. Please stick with us!

    How Is Angourie Rice's Professional Life Going? 

    20-year-old Angourie Rice began her acting career with her appearance on several short films, Hidden Cloud, and television advertisements. At the age of eleven, she played the leading role in Zak Hilditch's short Transmission and grabbed the industry's attention.

    Looking through her acting career, Rice was initially recognized after her role of Jane on the Nicole Kidman in the thriller film, The Beguiled (2017). In addition to that, she is known for her credits on Spider-Man: Far From Home, Black Mirror: Rachel, Jack, and Ashley.

    Later in 2018, Angourie starred in Every Day, directed by Michael Sucsy, and period drama Ladies in Black, directed by Bruce Beresford. From the successful running of these films, she won the Australian Academy of Cinema & Television Arts (AACTA) and the Film Critics Circle of Australia awards for best actress.

    What is Angourie Rice's Net Worth? How Much Does She Make?

    According to the online source, Angourie has a net worth of $3 million. She made such a huge wealth from her over a decade-long professional acting career. 

    The most significant work of Angourie includes Spider-Man, the comic of which was created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko in 1963. She also appeared in the feature adaptation of Jasper Jones, playing the primary role and in The Nice Guys, directed by Shane Black.

    For her performances in these movies, she received outstanding reviews from fans and critics. Rice won a Best Actress Award for her central role in Zak Hilditch's short Transmission at St. Kilda Film Festival in 2012.

    Is Angourie Rice Dating Anyone Recently?

    Angourie Rice, who is taking over Hollywood, does not have a boyfriend and is currently single. She is busy building up her career in the film sector and does not seem interested in love and romance for now.

    The Aussie teen resides with her mother, Kate Rice, father, Jeremy Rice, and a younger sister Kalliope Rice. Her parents have been married since 2000.

    Angourie's mom, Kate, born on June 2, 1969, is an actress and a writer well known for Ocean Star(2008), Home and Away(1998), and Trapped(2004). Similarly, Jeremy, Angourie's dad, is recognized for his work from his partner's write, Trapped, Ocean Star and Gorno: an American Tragedy(2003). 

    Angourie Rice: Early Life And Education

    Angourie Rice was born on January 1, 2001, to Kate Rice and Jeremy Rice in Sydney, Australia. She was named after a town where her grandmother lived in New South Wales.

    Angourie lived in Perth for five years and in Munich, Germany, before moving to Australia. Looking through her education, Rice attended Princes Hill Secondary College and graduated in 2018. 

    Angourie Rice is a student currently studying at Midtown School of Science and Technology. Further, she also serves as a co-host of Midtown News with her classmate, Jason Lionello.
