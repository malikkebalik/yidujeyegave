    Robert George Kardashian Wiki Biography

    Robert George Kardashian, was born on 22 February 1944, in Los Angeles, California USA, TO Armenian-American parents. He was a famous businessman and attorney, who came to real prominence when working as an attorney during the 1995 murder trial of Orenthal James (OJ – ‘Juice’) Simpson. What is more, Robert was known for being the father of Kourtney, Rob, Kim and Khloe Kardashians, who are now very popular. Unfortunately, Robert died of cancer when he was only 59 years old. There is no doubt that if Robert would have lived longer he would have achieved even more in his career as an attorney and would be happy to see his children creating families and fame of their own.

    So how rich was Robert Kardashian? It is authoritively estimated that Robert’s net worth was $30 million. The main source of this sum of money was, of course, from his work as an attorney. In addition to this, Robert’s other activities also added to this sum of money. Although his net worth will not become higher, he is remembered for his work and achievements.

    Robert’s parents were involved in the meat-packing business and were one of the most famous businesspeople in the area.
