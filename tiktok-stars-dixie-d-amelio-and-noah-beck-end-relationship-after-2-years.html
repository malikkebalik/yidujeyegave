    TikTok Stars Dixie DAmelio and Noah Beck End Relationship after 2 Years

    Dixie DAmelio and Noah Beck have decided to go their separate ways after a two-year relationship. The 21-year-old social media personality and the 21-year-old TikTok figure had previously broken up during an episode of The DAmelio Show, but later reconciled.

    31 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Dixie D’Amelio and Noah Beck have decided to go their separate ways after a two-year relationship.

    The 21-year-old social media personality and the 21-year-old TikTok figure had previously broken up during an episode of The D’Amelio Show, but later reconciled.

    Beck’s representatives recently confirmed to The New York Times that the couple has parted ways.

    “We can confirm that the pair are no longer together,” Beck’s representatives told the New York Times earlier this week. They added that despite the breakup, the former couple hopes to “remain close friends.”

    The news follows months of speculation about their relationship, sparked by Beck’s absence from D’Amelio’s 21st birthday party in Las Vegas and their dwindling presence together on TikTok.

    Addressing their “complicated” relationship, D’Amelio explained in an interview with Entertainment Tonight in September, “We are together, but keeping it offline. Obviously, it does make things more complicated keeping things offline, but at the end of the day there’s so much back-and-forth of trying to build a career and trying to figure out if we’re even happy.”

    She added, “Going from seeing each other every day in quarantine and being so busy and having schedules that we can’t align together with is very hard and very difficult,” and expressed hope for understanding from their followers.

    D’Amelio and Beck were first linked in 2020, and she had previously dated his fellow Sway House member Griffin Johnson. The couple publicly acknowledged their feelings for each other in August 2020 and officially started dating the following month.
