    Coi Leray, born Brittany Collins on May 11, 1997, is a multifaceted American personality known for her successful career as a rapper, singer, songwriter, and social media influencer. She hails from Boston, Massachusetts, USA, and is notably recognized as the daughter of the prominent American television personality, Raymond Leon Scott, famous as “Benzino”.

    Leray has made a mark with her music albums like Everythingcoz, EC2, and Now or Never, along with several EPs and singles. Her controversial single “No More Parties” stirred up a family dispute in March 2021 due to some contentious lyrics about her father.

    Wiki/Biography

    Born on May 11, 1997, in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, Coi Leray comes from a family deeply ingrained in the spotlight. She is known to follow the Christian faith and is currently 26 years old. Coi’s birth name is Brittany Collins, and she made the bold decision to drop out of high school at the age of 16 to pursue her passion for music. This early dedication has brought her widespread recognition as one of America’s most esteemed rappers and singers.

    Family and Ethnicity

    According to media sources, Coi Leray is of Cape Verdean and Puerto Rican descent, resulting in her multiracial background intersecting Black and Hispanic heritage.

    Parents and Siblings

    Coi Leray belongs to a family of public figures, with her father, Raymond Leon Scott (Benzino), being a well-known TV personality. While she maintains privacy about her mother’s identity, she has shared glimpses of her on her Instagram account. Leray has two brothers, Kwame (elder) and Taj (younger), and has chosen to focus primarily on her music career.

    Relationships & Boyfriends

    Being open-minded, Coi Leray enjoys socializing with her peers. Notably, she was in a relationship with the renowned rapper “Trippie Redd,” which began in 2019 and ended in 2020 due to undisclosed reasons. However, as of March 2022, there is no public information about her current relationship status, indicating that she may be single.

    Career in Music

    Starting her musical journey at a young age, Coi Leray’s decision to leave school at 16 paved the way for her flourishing music career. Her debut EP album, “Everythingcoz,” released in 2018, marked the beginning of her rise to prominence. Leray gained widespread attention with the viral success of singles like G.A.N and Pac Girl, along with her enthralling live performances and music videos.

    Coi Leray’s Net Worth in 2024

    As of February 2024, Coi Leray’s net worth stands at approximately USD 10-15 million, primarily derived from her music career and supplemented by her successful endeavors in the fashion industry. She also boasts a significant income from endorsements and her fashion clothing line, as well as her official website.

    Coi Leray and her Controversy

    Following the release of “No More Parties” in February 2021, Coi Leray found herself embroiled in a family controversy due to certain lyrics in the song. This sparked heated public discussions, including tweets from Coi herself expressing her feelings about the matter.
