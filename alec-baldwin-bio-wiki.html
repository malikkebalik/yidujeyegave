    Who is Alec Baldwin?

    Alec Baldwin is an American actor, writer, producer, and comedian. He is famous for his remarkable work of displaying convincing characters in just about every genre.

    Alec Baldwin: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

    Alec Baldwin was born on April 3, 1958, in Amityville, New York, United States. Currently, he is 63 years old. He is of English, Irish, Scottish, French-Canadian, and German descent and holds an American nationality.

    His birth name is Alexander Rae Baldwin III. He is the eldest son of Carol M. Baldwin(mother) and Alexander Rae Baldwin, Jr.(father), a high school history/social studies teacher and football coach. Baldwin has three young brothers, Daniel, William, Stephen, and two sisters, Beth and Jane.

    In which school did Alec Baldwin attend?

    He went to Alfred G. Berner High School in Massapequa and played football there under Coach Bob Reifsnyder. In New York City, he filled in as a table attendant at the disco Studio 54.

    From 1976 to 1979, he went to George Washington University. Afterward, he exchanged to New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts where he examined, among others, Geoffrey Horne and Mira Rostova at the Lee Strasberg Theater Institute. Later, he was recognized as an individual from the Actors Studio.

    Alec Baldwin: Professional Life and Career

    Alec had begun his career in 1980 when he was cast in the daytime soap opera The Doctors, followed by a role in the primetime soap Knot’s Landing from 1984 to 1985. In 1986, he made his Broadway debut in Joe Orton’s Loot, and he made his film debut a year later in the movie Forever Lulu. Afterward, he had shown his versatility as an actor in films including Beetlejuice, Working Girl, The Hunt for Red October, The Cooler, The Departed and Mission Impossible: Rogue Nation.

    Meanwhile, he has received two Emmy Awards and three Golden Globe nominations for his role as TV executive Jack Donaghy on the NBC sitcom 30 Rock and holds the record for most times hosting Saturday Night Live. Moreover, he has been honored with the awards like Florida Film Critics Circle Awards, Dallas-Fort Worth Film Critics Association Awards, Gemini Awards and much more. He won Florida Film Critics Circle Awards back in 2001 and that is also his only nomination for this award.
