    With February just around the corner and a month of brand-new K-Pop idol birthdays to celebrate, January is about to end. Here are more than 40 birthdays of male heroes you won’t want to miss!

    Doyoung

    Kim Dong-young, sometimes known as Doyoung (Korean), is a South Korean musician, actor, and host born on February 1st, 1996. The South Korean boy group NCT and its sub-units NCT U and NCT 127 are where he gained most of his fame.

    Kyuhyun, who turned 35 this year, was born on February 3, 1988. Cho Kyu-hyun, also known as Kyuhyun, is a South Korean singer, musical theater performer, and television personality. In May 2006, he made his debut as a brand-new member of the boy band Super Junior. He then joined Super Junior-K.R.Y. and Super Junior-M and was a former member of the SM Entertainment project group SM the Ballad.

    Nam Woo-Hyun, also known by his stage name Woohyun, is a South Korean singer, composer, and actor (Korean: born February 8, 1991). He is well known for being a part of the Infinite boy band from South Korea.
