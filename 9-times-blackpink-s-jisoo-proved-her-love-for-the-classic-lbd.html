    With sold-out stadium shows worldwide and records being broken one after the other, it’s safe to say that Blackpink is one of the most popular bands in the world. The four members, Jennie, Lisa, Rosé, and Jisoo, have staggered the world with not just their talent but also their incredible stage presence and style.

    Each Blackpink member has a strong sense of distinctive personal style, making them among the front-row occupants at major fashion events, become ambassadors for luxury fashion houses and strutting on the red carpets of the most elusive events in the world.

    With that being said, the thing that sets Jisoo’s fashion sense apart from the rest of the group is her affinity towards the classics. The world noticed this too, with the singer becoming a local ambassador for Dior in 2019 and becoming a global one in 2021. However, as of last year, Jisoo has become the face of Cartier.

    Over the years, we’ve seen her style evolve and her confidence as a style icon grow from look to look. The ‘Flower’ singer’s love for hyper-feminine silhouettes hasn’t changed but looking back at some of her most iconic fashion moments, we couldn’t help but notice her love for the classic LBD.

    Whether she’s attending the premiere of her on-screen debut or occupying the front row at Paris Fashion Week, you’d be hard-pressed to find a look by Jisoo that’s not a variation of a black mini dress. Here’s a round-up of some of our favourite Jisoo looks to give you the style inspiration you need.
