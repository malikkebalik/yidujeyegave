    Trish Stratus’ Biography

    Patricia Anne Stratigias, also known as Trish Stratus, was born on 18 December 1975, in Richmond Hill, Ontario, Canada. She comes from a Canadian family with Greek and Polish descent. Trish Stratus is a retired WWF/WWE wrestler, fitness model, and fitness guru. She had a successful wrestling career, winning seven WWE Women’s Championships and several other titles such as Diva of the Decade, WWE Hardcore Champion, and thrice WWE Babe of the Year. Trish Stratus’s net worth has significantly increased due to her accolades in wrestling, as well as her later work in television and the fitness industry.

    Sources estimate Trish Stratus’ net worth to be around $2.5 million, accumulated from her modeling career in the late ’90s and various activities in subsequent years.

    Trish Stratus’ Net Worth: $2.5 Million

    Even in her childhood, Stratus was a fan of wrestling and actively participated in various sports like field hockey and soccer during her time in York University. Her career took off when she was noticed while working as a receptionist at a local gym, leading to a successful career in fitness modeling, including appearing on the cover of the May 1998 issue of “MuscleMag International.”

    Trish Stratus’s net worth took a major boost when she began her professional wrestling career in the World Wrestling Federation. Over her wrestling career, she won multiple WWE Women’s Championships and gained widespread popularity, consequently increasing her net worth substantially. After retiring from wrestling, she found success as an actress and television personality, hosting her show “Stratusphere” on the TV channel “Travel + Escape” and making guest appearances in other productions. Additionally, she ventured into the fitness industry by opening her yoga studio, the “Stratusphere,” which received several awards, including the Top Choice Awards’ Best New Business in 2009.

    Trish Stratus married her childhood sweetheart, Ron Fisico, in 2006, and they reside in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, with their son.
