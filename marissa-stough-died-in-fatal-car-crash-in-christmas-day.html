    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Lebanon, PA – The community of Lebanon, PA, is grieving the devastating loss of 20-year-old Marissa Stough, who tragically passed away in a car accident on Christmas day. Marissa, on the brink of establishing herself as a sought-after beautician, had her promising future cut short by this heartbreaking incident, as lamented by a close friend.

    Marissa Stough’s untimely demise in the car crash was confirmed by Sinkhole Saloon and Grille, where she was an integral part of their team for several years. Acknowledging her versatility and dedication, the establishment expressed their deep connection with Marissa, highlighting her proficiency in various roles within their organization. They revered her as a cherished member of their work family, emphasizing her warmth and exceptional service to their guests.

    Hershey Blaze Track Club posted on Facebook,

    “It is with great sadness that we share the news of the untimely loss of a beautiful soul and former Blaze athlete. Marissa Stough of Palmyra lost her life tragically on Christmas Day. She will be remembered as such an incredibly sweet young lady. Please keep her family in your thoughts and prayers during this very difficult time. If you would like to help support the family please contact us.”

    Described as fiercely independent, Marissa had been living independently for years, tirelessly working multiple jobs to make ends meet. Her unwavering dedication and ability to juggle various roles underscored her determination and resilience.

    To support the family with the expenses for Marissa’s funeral, a GoFundMe account has been initiated. The heartfelt plea for contributions reflects the desire to honor Marissa with a fitting service, acknowledging her beautiful spirit and the impact she had on those around her.

    Details regarding Marissa Stough’s funeral arrangements and obituary will be shared by her family in the coming days, providing an opportunity for collective remembrance and tribute to a beloved individual whose life was tragically cut short.
