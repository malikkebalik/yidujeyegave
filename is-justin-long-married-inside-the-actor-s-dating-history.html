    Actor Justin Long seemingly reveals that he is married: More about his love life!  

    Justin Long is known for his roles in Jeepers Creepers, Accepted, Dodgeball, Going the Distance, Dreamland, and The Wave. Long is a fast-rising actor. In a career spanning over two decades, Long has entrenched a dynamic profile with his innumerable roles in hit comedies, rom-com, and horror flicks. Besides his career, Long found his perfect half!

    The actor's star-studded dating career is another factor that corroborates his A-list status. Born Justin Jacob Long, on June 2, 1978, in Fairfield, Connecticut, he began his acting career in 1999. In recent years, Long has also attempted to branch his repertoire by trying his hands at podcasts, television shows, and even filmmaking.   

    Is Justin Long Married?  

    Justin Long recently admitted being a husband on his podcast, Life is Short. More deets on Long's newly-announced marriage is still pending, but yes, the Dodgeball actor is apparently out of the market for good. 

    During his May 9 episode, Justin casually referred to Kate Bosworth, his longtime girlfriend, and fiancee of a month, as his wife. 

    The actor was actually sharing an anecdote about his 2022 horror movie, Barbarian when he casually slipped the exciting information. 

    "I was there while I was like really falling in love with my now-wife," the actor shared.

    If that doesn't confirm Justin's marital status then we don't know what will. The Going the Distance actor and Bosworth have been linked since 2021. They reportedly started as colleagues while filming for a movie in early 2021 when things turned romantic.

    Are Justin Long and Kate Bosworth Dating?

    Justin Long and his partner, Kate Bosworth, kept their budding relationships under wraps. Moreover, Bosworth was amid a separation from her former spouse, Michael Polish.   

    Long and the Blue Crush actress went off guards once Bosworth confirmed her split from Polish in the Fall. By early 2021, the twosome were madly and quite openly in love with each other.

    During his visit to Chelsea Handler's Dear Chelsea podcast, Long asserted that his relationship with Kate was a new and completely different experience than his previous ones.  

    "I've never experienced this. So it's something I want to protect and keep, you know?" Justin gushed. 

    Things haven't looked better for the twosome since, with loved-up posts and shout-outs coming up regularly, and flaunting about their relationship on every other interview.    

    Justin Long and his now-wife, Kate Bosworth, started dating amid the actress's split from her first husband, Michael Polish, in 2021! 

    But even a relationship as perfect and enviable as theirs have problems. Earlier last month, the He's Just Not That Into You actor opened up about facing challenges as a couple in his podcast. Justin revealed that he and Bosworth went to therapy and got engaged as they pledged to always work things out.  

    Get to Know Justin Long's Wife, Kate Bosworth!  

    Justin Long's wife, Kate Bosworth, 40, is yet to comment on her husband's revelation of what we believe was supposed to be a secret marriage, at least for a while.

    As we expect and await more stories about their wedding, let's get to know the blonde beauty who's taken Justin off his feet. Professionally known as Kate Bosworth, she was born Catherine Anne Bosworth, on January 2, 1983, in Los Angeles, California. 

    Bosworth is also an experienced horse rider, and that's how she landed her debut role in the 1998 film, The Horse Whisperer. Kate has since cemented a stellar profile with roles in mega-hits like Blue Crush, Wonderland, Beyond the Sea, The Domestics, and Netflix's The I-Land. 

    Justin and Kate co-starred in the 2022 horror film Barbarian. The actress is set to star in the upcoming The Locksmith. 

    Other High Profiled Affairs of Kate!

    Bosworth famously dated actor Orlando Bloom, who is now engaged to singer Katy Perry, from 2002 to 2005. She married director Michael Polish in August 2013 and called it quits with him in August 2021.

    Justin Long's dating history is just as stellar as his career. The Wave actor jumped into the Hollywood dating pool as early as 2003, he dated the stunning French model Olga Kurylenko.
