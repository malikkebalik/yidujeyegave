    Late Paul Walker’s only child, Meadow, is now engaged nearly 8 years after his death.

    The 22-year-old model made the announcement on Instagram on Monday, August 9, with a video of her flashing her engagement ring.

    Meadow went Instagram official with actor Louis Thornton-Allan just last month. It’s unclear how long they’ve been together before that.

    Meadow and Allan

    In early July, the actor posted a photo of him and Meadow cuddled together on a couch and smiling at each other.

    “Best friend,” Thornton-Allan wrote in the caption. Walker commented on the photo with, “My love.”

    Meadow Walker also shared a video of herself holding the actor’s face on Instagram in mid-July with the caption, “Hi.” 

    Meadow’s father, Paul Walker, popular for his role in the Fast & Furious movies, died on November 30, 2013, in a car crash.
