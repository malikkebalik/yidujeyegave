    Here are 71 amazing House of Cards quotes, including Frank Underwood Quotes through which you get a glimpse of the mindset of the US Politicians and politics in general.

    These quotes from House of Cards show you what happens in the power circles in the United States.

    The drama focuses on Frank Underwood, a ruthless congressman, a Democrat from South Carolina, and his equally ambitious wife, Claire Underwood.

    House of Cards is the story of Frank’s progression from being a House Majority Whip to Vice President of US and ultimately becoming the President of the United States of America.

    His wife, Claire, aided him in his pursuit of power through lies, betrayal, manipulation, and pragmatism. She goes on to become the President of the United States at the end of the season.

    The role of Francis Underwood, also known as Frank Underwood, is played brilliantly by Kevin Spacey in House of Cards.

    Despite being the anti-hero, he is the most popular character of the show. Robin Wright played the role of Frank Underwood’s wife, Claire Underwood.
