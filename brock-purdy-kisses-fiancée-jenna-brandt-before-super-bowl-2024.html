    San Francisco 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy shared a kiss with his fiancée, Jenna Brandt, during a sweet moment on the sidelines before playing in his first Super Bowl against the Chiefs on Sunday.

    Purdy greeted Brandt, who was sporting his No. 13 on her shirt, while she was standing on the sidelines with his family during warmups.

    The couple, who got engaged in July 2023, shared a sweet exchange before Purdy embraced his parents, Shawn and Carrie, and sister Whittney.

    Brandt has been a big supporter of Purdy long before he was dubbed “Mr. Irrelevant” as the last pick of NFL draft in 2022, the same year they made their relationship Instagram official.

    The future Mr. and Mrs. Purdy met while students at Iowa State University, where he played football and she was on the volleyball team and majored in Kinesiology.

    Purdy proposed on a dock in Saint Cloud, Florida last July.

    “My Jenna girl forever,” the quarterback wrote on Instagram at the time, including photos from their engagement. “I can’t wait to be your husband and grow together in Christ. Here’s to forever babe. I love you JB!”

    What we covered during Super Bowl 2024

    In a separate Instagram post, Brandt wrote, “WE GET TO DO LIFE TOGETHER…FOREVER! Brock Purdy – I love everything about you & your heart for Jesus more than any words can say.”

    Purdy and Brandt shared another sweet moment after the 49ers beat the Lions 34-31 in the NFC Championship game on Jan. 28.

    “The BEST feeling. God is good – all the time,” Brandt wrote on Instagram at the time, including a video of the couple kissing on the field at Ford Field in Detroit.

    Purdy led the 49ers to a 12-5 regular season record, his first full season as an NFL starter.
