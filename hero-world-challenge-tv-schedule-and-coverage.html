    Hero World Challenge TV schedule starts from November 30 until December 3. Golf Channel and NBC will be providing live coverage of the tournament.

    The annual golf tournament hosted by legendary golfer Tiger Woods is part of the PGA Tour but does offer the participants any FedEx Cup points or official money. Although part of the tour's schedule, it is considered an unofficial event.

    The tournament was first held in Grayhawk Golf Club in January 2000 and the second was also held in the same year in December. The tournament took place in Sherwood Country Club and was home to the event until 2013.

    Since then, the event has been scheduled for December and was initially known as Williams World Challenge (2000-2001), Target World Challenge (2002-2007), Chevron World Challenge (2008-2011), and Northwestern Mutual World Challenge (2012-2013).

    The grand stage has been set once again and in only a few days' time, Albany will be home to some of the best golfers in the world. The tournament will be a benefit for the Tiger Woods Foundation.

    Tiger Woods' name is synonymous with golf and to the liking of the fans, he has announced that he will be taking part in this year's edition of the tournament.

    He is the most successful golfer in this particular tournament having won it five times. Currently ranked 4th in the world, Viktor Hovland is the defending champion having won consecutive titles.

    Tom Lehman was the inaugural champion in 2000 when he beat David Duval by a three-stroke margin and took home a million dollars as the winner's share.

    Since the tournament took place twice that year, Davis Love III won his first Hero World Challenge title beating none other than Tiger Woods by two strokes. Love went on to win another title three years later.

    Graeme McDowell has also won the event twice in his career in 2010 and 2012. He beat Woods for the first title and Keegan Bradley for the second. 

    Where Is The Hero World Challenge Played?

    The golf tournament is to be played at the Albany Golf Course once again in December 2023. It has been home to the event since 2015.

    Albany Bahamas is truly a golfer's paradise with a mixture of the best of links and desert features which makes it one of the most beautiful courses in the world. The course also boasts world-class practice facilities, performance facilities, and an elite golf academy.

    Albany Golf Club location sits on the southwestern end of the island of New Providence in the Bahamas. Albany is a luxury resort community on the island.

    The community was created by none other than Tiger Woods, Tavistock Group, and Ernie Els. The golf course was also designed by Els along with Greg Letsche in 2010.

    Albany features an 18-hole par-72 championship golf course with a length of 7,414 yards (6,779 m). It also boasts a luxury boutique hotel, a spa and fitness center, a family water park, an adult pool, a variety of restaurants and bars, a children's clubhouse, and a 71-slip mega-yacht marina.

    The golf course will have some of the world's best players taking part and give the fans and viewers an exclusive tournament experience.

    How To Watch Hero World Challenge?

    Golf Channel will provide the 2023 Hero World Challenge live coverage for all four rounds. NBC will also be providing coverage of the third and fourth rounds.

    Fans will not have to worry about missing out on the star-studded golf tournament in the Bahamas. Here is the schedule for the upcoming event:

    Golf Channel

    • November 30 - 1:30 pm - 4:30 pm
    • December 1 - 10:00 am
    • December 2 - 12 pm to 2:30 pm
    • December 3 - 11:30 am - 1:30 pm

    NBC 

    • December 2 - 2:30 pm - 5 pm
    • December 3 - 1:30 pm - 4:30 pm

    Another way to watch the Hero World Challenge is to be actually present in the Bahamas and watch the action as it follows.

    Hero World Challenge tickets can be bought at TicketMaster for just $100 for a single ticket and an intimate tournament experience.

    With the tournament's out-of-the-world hospitality and also a lovely getaway for fans, the event would be an experience unlike any other. You could get a chance to watch your favorite golfers play right in front of you.
