    Brooklynn Kimberly Prince, also known as Brooklynn Prince who is an American actress. She flaunts her outstanding acting skills as she has worked in various movies and TV series. The young actress has played unique roles as she has work in the horror film i.e., The Turning (2020). Moreover, she is a victor of several awards and also earned a number of nominations.

    She was born in Florida, USA where she raised and grew up with siblings and family. She had a passion for acting since childhood as she wanted to be an actress. Brooklynn Prince credits her parents who have been supportive throughout her career.

    Career Journey

    At the age of 2, Brooklynn Prince started her career with the help of her parents, and she appeared in print & screen advertisements including Parenting, Chuck E. Cheese’s, and others as well.

    She made her acting debut in 2017 with the movie Robo-Dog: Airborne. In this movie, she played the role of Mira Perry for which she got a lot of recognition & fame.

    She has done many roles in various films and became widely popular. Brooklynn Prince played in the film i.e., The Florida Project (2017) as Moonee opposite some other great actors i.e., Bria Vinaite, Willem Dafoe.

    She rose to fame when she appeared in The Angry Birds Movie 2, and The Lego Movie 2: The Second Part for which she got critical praise and recognition.

    Since then, she started her career professionally and worked in several films and television series for which she received critical acclaim and immense love and praise from her millions of followers and fans across the world. Undoubtedly, the young star has won millions of hearts with her wonderful acting skills.

    In 2020, presently, she features in Home Before Dark which is a television show based on a real story (Hilde Lysiak). In the TV show, the story revolves around a 9-year-old journalist.

    Achievements

    She is the winner of several awards and honors including the Critics’ Choice Movie Awards for the category of Best Young Performer for the movie The Florida Project. Also, she won the Alliance of Women Film Journalists Awards for the category of Best Breakthrough Performance. Among others, she won the Seattle Film Critics Society for Best Youth Performance and the list goes on. Interestingly, she has earned a lot of nominations for her best work.
